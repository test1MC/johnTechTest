Historic Roll Rates

The Historic Roll Rates module displays historic roll, switch, stay, and attrition percentages of term products across portfolio dimensions, and is designed to provide insights to customer decisions at the time of maturity.

Note: PriceTek's Roll methodology looks a month beyond the maturity month to re-adjust for any grace period decisions, so data points in the latest month are considered tentative until that adjustment, identified with the different chart shapes. It also identifies rate relationships between balances that rolled or switched terms.

Metrics displayed on the chart are:

Percent Roll: Percent of balances that stay within the bank; comprised of both Percent Stay and Percent Switch.

Percent Stay: Percent of balances that stay within the bank, and stay within their original term.

Percent Switch: Percent of balances that stay within the bank, but switch to a different term or to a different product.

Percent Attrite: Percent of balances that leave the bank altogether.

Common Use Cases for this Module

 

  • Identify if customers of any specific segmentation show differences in term balance retention behavior
  • Track changes in term retention over time through changing rate environments
  • Chart insight into customer behavior at term product maturity (e.g., CD Attrition spikes following two spikes in earlier months - one in which customers stayed in the same term CD followed by one where balances switched to other products).

 

How do I use this Module?

 

The Historic Roll Rates module displays historic roll, switch, stay, and attrition percentages of term products by geography and/or by time . It also identifies rate relationships between balances that rolled or switched terms.

1.    Access the Bank Data section (choose Historic Roll Rates), and use the multi-select Data Filters on the left side to narrow down your institution's unique portfolio segmentation (i.e., Product Group, Term Bucket, etc.).

The default starting selections are All for all Segmentation Filters.

2.    Choose the Start Date and End Date you want to test.

Note: You must click to apply changes.

3.    Select a metric and relevant choices to display, aggregate, and/or group the output using the applicable Chart Format drop-downs - X-Axis Value, External Ref Rate, and Metric Aggregation.

This organizes your data for different views - and each view tells a different story.

Nuances to this module

  • From the Legend on the right side of the page, toggle on and off any of the metrics displayed. Click All to display all or None to turn off all of the metrics (and then turn back on one at a time).
  • Click the Table tab to view the underlying data.

Tip: The Table tab displays rates at last renewal.

 

Methodology and Definitions

  Methodology  
  Refer to the Bank Data Methodology for details.
  Data Dictionary
  Reference Rates External reference rates that are aggregations of peer advertised pricing. Click here to see in-scope reference rates, and their definitions.
  % of First Maturity Percent of balances in the aggregated cell that are made up of term accounts that have not yet had a maturity event since they were first opened, and are thus considered to be at 'first maturity'.
  CD Renewal Status Used to describe if a term product has experienced a maturity event since account opening or is still at 'first maturity.' Depending on your configuration, there may be multiple available CD Renewal Statuses.
  Percent Attrite The % of balances that leave the bank altogether
  Percent Roll The % of starting balance that partially or entirely stays with the bank (i.e., does not attrite)
  Percent Stay A subset of Percent Roll, the % of balances that stay within the bank, and stay within their original term. (Percent Stay + Percent Switch = Percent Roll)
  Percent Switch A subset of Percent Roll, the % of balances that stay with the bank but switch to another product/term. (Percent Stay + Percent Switch = Percent Roll)
