Acquisition Elasticity

The Acquisition Elasticity Dashboard displays the balances per branch that peers are acquiring at each pricing level.

Hover your mouse over the screenshot below to display pop-up content detailing sections of the screen:

Use the drop-down arrows to change the data set.

  • The only Institutions available on this dashboard are your institution and the All Banks peer set in order to protect the anonymity of participant banks.

  • The Product drop-down allows you to choose rate sensitive products (e.g., savings accounts, as well as short and long-term CDs).
  • The Term Buck drop-down allows you to drill-down further into Term Buckets (e.g., 0-23 months, 24+ months) for CDs. This filter is dependent on the Product filter.

Note: The Term Bucket drop-down is not applicable to savings accounts (NA).

The Blue circles indicates your institution while the orange circles indicates All Banks.

Hover over any of the circles to display the Acquisition balance per branch at the Acquisition Weighted Rate, as well as the date of that acquisition.

The Y-Axis displays the Acquisition balances per Branch.

The X-Axis displays the Absolute Rate (Acquisition Weighted Rate).

Use the Slider to narrow down the historical data set - up to 36 months of data is available.

Note: If you do not see All Banks data for the most recent month, it is because that data is not yet available. Use the Slider to pull back to the prior month until All Banks data is displayed.

 

Common Use Cases for this Module
  • Am I able to acquire more or less balances than peers at the same rate level? Are my customers more or less elastic than peer customers?
  • I have not priced aggressively in CDs in recent history; what rate do I need to offer to drive CD acquisitions?
  • Are peers pricing significantly higher than me on any products, and are they successful at driving more acquisitions?

 

How do I use this Module?

 

1.    From the Benchmarking header, choose Analytics.

The CDA Workbook page is displayed.

2.    Click the 2a. Acquisition Elasticity dashboard.

Data in the scatter plot indicate acquisition elasticity for your institution against your peers for rate sensitive products: savings and short and long term CDs.

3.    Optional: Use the Product drop-down arrow to change the product (e.g., Savings, Short and Long-term CDs).

Note: The Institution drop-down is essentially disabled for this Dashboard. Only All Banks can be displayed for comparison.

4.    Optional: Use the Term Buck drop-down arrow to drill-down further into Term Buckets (e.g., 0-23 months, 24+ months) for those products to which it relates (e.g., CDs).

Note: For Products without terms (e.g., Checking, Savings), the default is NA (Not Applicable).

5.    Optional: Use the Slide bar to narrow the date range from the 36 month period.

Note: If you do not see All Banks data for the most recent month, it is because that data is not yet available. Use the Slider to pull back to the prior month until All Banks data is displayed.

6.    Hover over bar to view metric details for the selected account tenure.
