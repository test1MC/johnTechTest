CDA Monthly Summary

The two CDA Monthly Summary reports provide a one-stop dashboard that displays each of the other CDA Dashboards as a monthly overview (compiled on the last business day of each month).

There are two (2) CDA Monthly Summary reports - one that provides per Branch data and one that provides Bank-wide standard data.

Note: You can access the CDA Monthly Summary (Standard) report and still view per Branch data online using the drop-down fields on those reports that have them. However, the easiest way to print both sets of data is to access each summary and download as a PDF.

Hover your mouse over the screenshot below to display pop-up content detailing sections of the screen:

Click Download and choose Download PDF to download the full multi-page report.

From the popup dialog box, to download all reports, the Include drop-down should be set to This Story. For best printing, click the Orientation drop-down and choose Landscape.

It will take 15-30 seconds to download.

You can also choose to tab through the reports one at a time.

Some of the reports have drop-down fields to choose specific Products, Metrics, etc.

 

Common Use Cases for this Module
  • Access a monthly summary of all CDA reports in one place, and download the report for distribution.

 

How do I use this Module?

 

1.    From the Benchmarking header, choose Analytics.

The CDA Workbook page is displayed.

2.    Click either the CDA Monthly Summary (per Branch) or CDA Monthly Summary (Standard) dashboards.

The report is displayed with (currently) 13 pages you can tab through.

3.    Optional: Use the Download drop-down arrow to choose Download PDF.

.

4.    To download all CDA reports: The Include drop-down should be set to This Story.
5.    Optional: For best print results, click the Orientation drop-down and choose Landscape.
6.    Click Create PDF.

It will take 15-30 seconds to download.

 

Related Dashboards

  Category Dashboard Description
  Summary 1. Summary Metrics Displays the overview of key metrics covering portfolio composition, flow of funds, rate distribution, and time series balance growth.
  Acquisition Pricing 2a. Acquisition Elasticity Displays the balances per branch that peers are acquiring at each pricing level.
  2b. mCOF Summary Contains information on each month of promotional pricing for savings products, such as rate, cannibalization, and mCOF (the "all in" cost of promotional pricing).
  2c. mCOF Over Time Displays the “all in” cost of acquiring an additional dollar of promotional balances over time.
  Portfolio Pricing 3a. Observed Betas Displays bank and peers have increased their acquisition and portfolio rates in response to increase Fed Funds rates.
  3b. Growth and Rates Displays both portfolio and acquisition growth and rates.
  3c. Growth & Rates by Region Displays both portfolio and acquisition growth and rates by region.
  3d. Rate Dispersion Displays portfolio and acquisition balances by rate (both granularly and into rate buckets).
  Portfolio Management 4a. Portfolio Makeup Over Time Displays portfolio and acquisition balances by product, term bucket, balance tier, or rate bucket (over time).
  4b. Balances by Account Age and Rate Bucket Displays balance by account age tranche and rate bucket.
  4c. CD Maturity Schedule Displays forward looking maturity information for bank compared to peers.
  Balance Movements and Retention 5a. Flow of Funds Waterfall Breaks down the balance change over a specific date range into various flows (e.g. acquisition, attrition), and shows where the bank is underperforming vs. overperforming.
  5b. Balance Flows Over Time Displays balance growth by month, and breaks down differences between bank and peers by product or flow.
