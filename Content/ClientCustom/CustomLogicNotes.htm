Demobank
Custom Logic Notes

  • Negative balance accounts are considered "open" but are overwritten to have a value of $0.01 to remain in the dataset, which may cause some discrepancies with internal reporting at the bank
  • Some Consumer product balances may show up in the "Small Business" Line of Business - this is due to their branch assignment. Any accounts associated with a "Business" branch will be mapped to the "Small Business" LOB, regardless of product

 

If there are no custom logic notes displayed above, please reach out to your Client Services team , or email your request to pricetek_support@novantas.com.
