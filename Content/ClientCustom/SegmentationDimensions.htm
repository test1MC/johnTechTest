Segmentation Dimensions

 

 
  Demobank
Segmentation Dimensions
Dimension Name Description Sample Values
Channel Designates Branch or Internet based accounts Branch, Direct
Line of Business Line of business associated with the account and product Consumer, Small Business, Private
Segment Customer CLV segment applied by DemoBank's marketing team Rewards Plus, VIP, etc
Pricing Region High level geographic identifier, assigned at the multi-state region level North East, South, North Central, etc
Pricing Market Most granular geographic identifier, aligned with DemoBank's sales regions Capeside, North Rockside, etc
Product Group Financial instruments offered Savings, Money Market, etc
Product Name Customer facing product names Step Rate CD, Silver Checking, Super Saver MMA, etc
Tier Bucket Balance tier assigned from that period's account level balances $0-$24,999, varies by product and line of business (note the only option available in this drop-down may be 'All' if upstream selections have different Tier Bucket breaks associated)
Term Bucket For term products only, the bucketed term, in months, of that product's maturity schedule 0-9 Months, 10-15 Months, etc
Term Actual CD term, in months 6, 7, 8, 9, etc
CD Renewal Status Applies to term products only. First Maturity represents products which have not yet matured, Multiple Maturities is 2+ maturity events associated with that account First Maturity, Multiple Maturities
