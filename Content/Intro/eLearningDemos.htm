Less Than 90 Second Demos

A series of short demos - all under 90 seconds - are being created, starting in 2019, to augment the Novantas eHelp and longer eLearning Demos. These demos ask and answer business questions by leveraging PriceTek Deposits modules.

 
 

 
Investigating Attrition

60 sec.

  • Use the Balance Flows module to find dates that attrition is unusually high in order to investigate the causes.
   Investigating Cannibalization

90 sec.

  • Start with the Balance Flows module to find Switch Out numbers and then move into the Switch Balances module to investigate further.
   Using Favorites

86 sec.

  • Favorite any analytic module so that you can re-access it again, share it with colleagues, and/or add it a Report Package.

PriceTek Deposits 2019.1 Release

45 min., April 2019

The latest release includes the migration of the Vintage Analytics section and core elements of Market Analytics to the central platform. Additionally, this release introduces the Balance Waterfall module, an interactive Scenario E-Learning Video, and other enhanced usability and aesthetic improvements.

The webinar includes a presentation on the elements of this release and the overall objectives of our broader roadmap, as well as a live product demonstration.
