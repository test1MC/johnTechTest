Ref Rates (Market)

The Ref Rates (Market) module displays a rate summary for a selected Pricing Market showing reference rate data for any selected product group over time, as well as individual competitor rates, client rates, macro rates (i.e., Fed Funds,) etc. As a drill-down to the Reference Rates (Market) module, data on this page is available for only one price cell at a time (i.e., one product, market, tier bucket, term bucket, etc.). For that cell, however, the module allows a time series view of all movements within a selected time frame - and the ability to compare the movement of your bank rate, reference rates, macro rates, and individual competitor rates all together.

Common Use Cases for this Module

 

  • For a selected time period, how has a particular product (e.g., CDs) in a particular Tier Bucket and/or Term Bucket changed relative to the WAMP or Weighted Max over time by month?
  • How has the rate of a particular competitor moved relative to the market moves?
  • What competitors have been driving the WAMP changes?
  • In the most competitive markets, what competitors have been driving the high Weighted Max rate?

 

How do I use this Module?

 

The Ref Rates (Market) module displays a rate summary for a selected Pricing Market showing reference rate data for any selected product group over time, as well as individual competitor rates, client rates, macro rates (i.e. Fed Funds,) etc.

1.    Access the Market Data section (choose Ref Rates (Market), and use the multi-select Data Filters on the left side to narrow down your institution's unique portfolio segmentation (i.e., Product Group, Term Bucket, etc.).

The default starting selections are All for all Segmentation Filters.

2.    Choose the Start Date and End Date you want to test.

Note: You must click to apply changes.

3.    Select one of the Reference Rates available to your institution.

Click here for a list of your in-scope reference rates and their definitions.

4.    If desired for comparison, click the Macro Rates drop-down.

A dialog box is displayed and a set of macroeconomic rates available to your institution are displayed.

5.    Click the check boxes for the Macro rates you want to include.

Note: These options are specific to your institution, so please talk to your Client Services Representatives if you’d like any changes here.

6.    To add individual competitors, click the Competitor Rates drop-down.

A dialog box is displayed and a set of competitors specific to your institution are displayed.

7.    Click the check boxes for the competitors you want to include. You can access subsets of competitors by toggling on and off the Large, Small, and Direct bank check boxes. You can also click All or None, if desired.
8.    When you are ready, click .

Nuances to this module

  • From the Legend on the right side of the page, toggle on and off any of the metrics displayed. Click All to display all or None to turn off all of the metrics (and then turn back on one at a time).
  • Click the Table tab to view the underlying data.
