Data Validation

Each time Novantas processes new data from the automated monthly, weekly, or daily data transmissions, a robust suite of validations and checks run in the background to ensure the data is valid and ready to be incorporated into PriceTek.

A large number of these processes are monitored by your support and configuration team at Novantas with a subset available to your institution's PriceTek users for verification. Through the Data Validation module, your institution is provided details to ensure that any critical errors or abnormalities are caught quickly, and made aware of any important Business Alerts to investigate.

You will receive an in application Alert about any new Validations that result in a status other than 'OK', appearing through the alerts bell at the top right corner of PriceTek Deposits:

Data Validations are displayed in multiple categories:

  • Data Validations: These are alerts that may be indicative of a data file abnormality, mapping issue (such as new products appearing), or a large enough change in a standard metric to warrant review.
  • Business Alerts: These are alerts that are meant to proactively direct your attention to certain segments of the portfolio in which new data is indicative of a recent or upcoming significant event that may require attention from the Product team.  Examples include alerts on high levels of attrition, or large upcoming term product maturity bubbles. Note: Custom alerts can be created on request, please reach out to your Client Success team to discuss this option!

The Acknowledged column allows you to identify data that has already been investigated or acted upon so that your institution users can easily coordinate across team members.

Status indicators are as follows:
Status Definition

OK

 Check has passed and all is okay - this is just a notification that the check has run
Note Check identified data or business change of interest for users to investigate and understand
Warning An alert tripped which indicates an abnormality that may indicate a data quality issue
  Critical A definite problem exists with the data, processing may have been halted or impacted, and need to be repeated
