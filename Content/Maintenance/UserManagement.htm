User Management

The User Management module allow the designated administrator-level users within your bank to:

  • View active product users
  • Add new users
  • Delete users
  • Edit user privileges
  • Reset a user's password

 

Adding a New User
1.    In the top right side of the blue bar above the main table, click .

The Add New User dialog box is displayed (right).

2.    Type the Email Address of the new user. This will also be their user name at login.
3.    Type a First Name and Last Name.
4.    Click the Role drop-down and choose:

Full Access User - The user will have access to all PriceTek modules that your institution has licensed.

Business Intelligence User - The user will have access to all Analytics modules only, but will not have access to either Scenarios or Optimization (if licensed).

Client Admin - Your PriceTek administrators; will also be able to add & edit users, delete users, change roles, etc.

Any other role available is client-specific. To add new Role levels, please reach out to your Client Success team members.

5.    Click .

 

Deleting User(s)
1.    Under the checkbox column on the far left side of the table, click to place a check mark for the user(s) to delete.
2.    Click .
3.    From the popup dialog box, click to confirm.
 

 

Editing User Privileges
1.    To change the Role of an individual user, under the Role Name column, double-click the listed role for a particular user you want to change (e.g., change from Full Access User to Client Admin).

A drop-down of available roles is displayed.

2.    Click the drop-down arrow and choose the new role for the user. Your change is saved automatically saved.

Assigning Roles to Multiple Users at Once

1.    Under the checkbox column on the far left side of the table, click to place a check mark for the desired user(s)
2.    Click .
3.    From the popup dialog box, click the Role drop-down and choose the desired role for selected user(s).
4.    Click to confirm.
 

 

Resetting Passwords

If a User forgets their password, Client Admin users can reset it. Users will receive an email with instructions on how to choose a new one.

1.    Under the checkbox column on the far left side of the table, click to place a check mark for the desired user(s).
2.    Click .
3.    From the popup dialog box, click to confirm.

Note: Users have the ability to do this self service from the login screen as well.
