PS: Custom Analytics

The Pricing Strategy Analytics module displays Novantas stock dashboards to use as a starting point for inspiration, as well as any custom dashboards you or your group have created within the Pricing Strategy section.

Click to view a list of available datasets to begin building your own custom dashboard. For more refer to Using Freeform Analytics .

A number of dashboards have been created in the Pricing Strategy section that provide Acquisition balances, Acquisition relative Rates and Flows by Market dashboards.

More dashboards will be added when available.

Dashboards created by your group using Freeform Analytics are displayed below the Novantas-created dashboards (not displayed).

Click a dashboard to display details.
