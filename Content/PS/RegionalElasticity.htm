Regional Elasticity

The Regional Elasticity module allows you to compare pricing elasticity by channel, line of business, product group, tier buckets, and term buckets for your bank regions in order to find potential locations that respond well to an increase in rate. The Y axis represents acquisition predicted a the minimum rate.  The X axis represents incremental acquisition expected when raising rates from the minimum rate to the maximum rate.

Note: Predictions are for a one-month time period.

Common Use Cases for this Module

 

This module is intended to be used as a "quick" Scenario reference - i.e., "if we were to change rates from X to Y, what geographies would be most impacted? Where would we expect to see substantial lift, vs little change? Where would we be acquiring a high amount of balances already at the min rate that would also become repriced?
